Division Collisions (radius ∝ √n)
New rule (prevents “everything → 2”): on collision, L=max(a,b), S=min(a,b). Compute q=⌊L/S⌋ and r=L mod S.
• The colliding larger circle becomes r (remainder).
• The quotient q splits off as a new circle (if q>1).
• Any circle with n≤1 disappears.
